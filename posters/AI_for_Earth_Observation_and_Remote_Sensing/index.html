AI for Earth Observation & Remote Sensing

How AI transforms extraction and interpretation of satellite data

Eleanor Buchanan, Vinay Lakshman, ... — International Space University • Poster track: Earth Observation

NASA
ISU

Abstract

Remote sensing provides high-volume, high-frequency views of Earth. Modern AI models accelerate the conversion of raw satellite observations into timely, actionable insights — from automated land-cover mapping to near-real-time disaster response. This poster highlights methods, results, and case studies demonstrating measurable improvements in speed, accuracy and scale.

Key Methods

  • Data fusion across optical, radar, and thermal sensors
  • Convolutional and transformer-based models for segmentation and change detection
  • Self-supervised pretraining on global archives to reduce labeled-data needs
  • Uncertainty estimation and domain adaptation for transfer across sensors

Applications

  • Crop health and yield forecasting (NDVI & phenology)
  • Flood and wildfire rapid mapping for emergency response
  • Urban change detection and infrastructure monitoring
  • Coastal erosion and sea-level trend analysis
