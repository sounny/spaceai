Implementation Roadmap

2025
Proof of Concept
Starcloud launches first commercial orbital data center with NVIDIA H100 GPU. Axiom Space deploys two ODC nodes in LEO. Google begins Project Suncatcher development.
2026-2027
Technology Demonstration
Starcloud-2 mission with GPU cluster. Google launches two prototype satellites. Commercial viability studies by Thales Alenia Space and ASCEND project.
2028-2030
Scale-Up Phase
First 50kW proof-of-concept deployment. Launch costs drop below $200/kg. Multiple companies begin commercial operations with micro data centers.
2031-2035
Commercial Deployment
Gigawatt-scale orbital data centers operational. Cost parity with terrestrial facilities achieved. Major cloud providers offer orbital computing services.
2036-2040
Mass Adoption
Most new data centers deployed in space. Lunar and deep space computing infrastructure. Orbital data centers become the primary platform for AI workloads.
2040+
Future Vision
Interplanetary computing network. Space-based manufacturing of data center components. Complete decoupling of digital growth from Earth's environmental constraints.
